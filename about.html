WHY FUSA?

We discoverd that the challenges of seeking investment opportunities in Africa include but not limited to: Limited access to information of financial securities, Loss of time spent on investment research (time value of money), Double remittance charges incurred, Risk of loss from FX instability etc.
That is why at fusa we try to bridge these challenges by creating a means where people within and outside africa are able to make investments all over the world as well as send and receive money without thinking of how to go about the FX. People are also able to save the time spent in converting currencies as well as the cost.
FUSA is the MOST advanced customer centric African financial securities investment platform that eases the process/challenges of seeking investment opportunities in Africa.
